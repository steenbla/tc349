- Our Story -


He was a handsome International Relations major, she a simple student of Political Theory and Constitutional Democracy. The first day of their sophomore year at Michigan State University was a fateful one as they both moved into rooms on the same floor of North Case Hall. While many would never have foreseen the love that would blossom between Paige and Chris, due to their strikingly differing political ideologies, the two began a journey together one romantic fall night after MSU destroyed UofM in football that it turned out would stretch far beyond college and into a lifetime together.

We are both currently working and living in Annapolis Maryland & counting down the days until our wedding back home, in Michigan.
